Real-World Project

HW Dental — Marketing Analytics

Uncovering how a growing Columbus dental chain can better reach its target populations through data-driven insights.

Live Client · Central Ohio

This project uses a de-identified dataset of 4,546 new patients across HW Dental's Clintonville, Polaris, Sawmill, and Westerville branches — capturing demographics, insurance types, referral sources, geographic distribution, and communication preferences.

My analysis focuses on two of the clinic's primary target populations: Medicaid recipients and Spanish-speaking patients (identified via language field and a name-based Spanish Likelihood model). The goal is to translate raw data into actionable marketing recommendations.
